About

Tivasoft

TIVASOFT AFRICA is a software development company located in the garden city of PortHarcourt, the capital of Rivers State, Southern Nigeria.

At Tivasoft Africa, we are a team of ICT and business enthusiasts, that are passionate and committed to building the best quality software's, web designs and other brand promoting ICT solutions to enhance growth, efficiency and productivity of African business. We are a team of experts determined to build affordable, available and accessible apps and sites for promotion and value creation of African businesses. We are an African company, built for African's, developing Africa by African's. We are here to bring African businesses to the global stage through ICT.

We understand the African business environment and our priority is to give our clients a competitive edge in the market place. Creativity is at the center of our goals, that's why we don't just register businesses for our clients, build applications that ease their service delivery and enhance efficiency and effectiveness but we also get to the length of providing real time business productivity solutions to other real time market place challenges that is dynamic to help our clients become the best.
